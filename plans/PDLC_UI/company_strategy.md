## Thesis (what we are)

**Wyzetalk is an employee engagement and workforce communications platform for organisations with large frontline / blue-collar / deskless populations** — not “office-only” employee apps as the primary persona.

We help employers **reach, inform, listen to, and activate** workers who do not sit at a desk: operational comms, culture and recognition, lightweight feedback and campaigns, and **mobile-first** experiences where those workers actually are.

---

## Strategic “in bounds” (examples)

- Comms and content **to** frontline teams (news, shifts, safety, change programmes).
- **Listening** and pulse-style engagement tied to workforce outcomes.
- **Recognition**, community, and culture mechanics suited to distributed / non-desk work.
- Integrations and workflows that **reduce friction** between HQ and the frontline (without replacing the core HRIS as the system of record for payroll/benefits unless explicitly a product bet).

---

## Strategic “out of bounds” or red-flag patterns (examples)

- **Unrelated consumer or personal-finance products** (e.g. a standalone **personal budgeting app** for end users) — *example you used for **off-strategy** ideation*.
- Bets that **redefine the company** away from workforce/employer value (generic B2C social, unrelated fintech) without explicit Steerco **strategy exception**.
- **One-off client customs** packaged as product **without** a line to the thesis above — treat as **deal customisation** or **park**, not silent product strategy.

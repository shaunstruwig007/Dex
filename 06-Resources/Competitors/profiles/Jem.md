**Core bundle (as of 2026-04):** EWA (SmartWage) · payslip delivery · financial education · Jem Mobile (device + data via payroll deduction) · employer-side HR comms

## Strategic frame (WT counter-thesis)

Jem wins on the **worker's wallet** — device, data, pay, savings. WT's counter-thesis is to win on the **employer's insight** — who's trained, who's compliant, who's engaged, what messages landed. These are not the same product. The risk is that Jem's financial bundle lowers device barriers enough that the employer's next question becomes "why do I need WT on top of Jem?" — WT's answer must be a measurably richer layer of engagement, compliance evidence, and advisory intelligence.

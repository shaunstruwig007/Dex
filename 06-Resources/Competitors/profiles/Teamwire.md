# Teamwire

## Snapshot

- **Category:** **Secure business messenger** — enterprise **mission-critical** communications (EU **GDPR**–first, **sovereign hosting**, **audit** and **emergency** features). Positioned explicitly as a **WhatsApp alternative**, **Microsoft Teams supplement** (data-sovereignty / US **CLOUD Act** framing), and **Slack alternative**.
- **Focus:** **Public safety** (police, fire, rescue, military-adjacent, security services), **critical infrastructure** (energy, transport, telco, banks/insurance), **healthcare**, **government / municipalities** — not “HR employee app” as primary frame, but **operational + compliant messaging**.
- **HQ:** **Munich, Germany** (Teamwire GmbH); operates in **German** and **English** markets per site.
- **Origins:** Enterprise messaging player from **~2014** era (Munich); parent/history linked to **grouptime** lineage in third-party databases — treat **scale stats** below as **marketing/press**, re-verify in deals.
- **Scale (directional):** Site and references cite **250,000+** daily users and references from **Bavarian Police**, **healthcare**, **municipalities**, **energy**, and **security technology** firms — see [Success stories](https://teamwire.eu/en/resources/).
- **Certifications / compliance (claimed):** **ISO 27001**, **TÜV Rheinland**, **BSI C5** hosting, **GDPR**, **NIS-2**, **DORA**/BaFin-relevant messaging for financial buyers — [Security narrative](https://teamwire.eu/en/).

## Product

- **Core UX:** **1:1, group, broadcast** chat; rich media; **read receipts**; reactions; **@mentions**; **voice/video** (up to ~30 participants per marketing); **screen share**; **pin/archive** inbox.
- **Mission-critical layer:** **Emergency** flows — **SOS** with location, **alerts** that can **break silent mode**, **highlighted** announcements, **live/static maps**, **AR** / map POIs, **ArcGIS** integration; **advanced polls**; **chat admin** / moderation / export.
- **Directory & admin:** Org directory, LDAP, **CSV** user import, **API** + bots, **professional dashboard**, **MDM/AppConfig**, **audit logs**, **audit-proof archiving**, mobile app management (BYOD and fully managed).
- **Hosting:** **On-premise**, **German public cloud**, **private cloud** — **Made & hosted in Germany** as primary trust story.
- **South Africa go-to-market:** **Official regional partnership with [Sentiv](./Sentiv.md)** — “**Teamwire** … **delivered in South Africa through Sentiv**” for public safety, emergency services, municipalities, and **frontline** operational teams (per [LinkedIn partnership activity](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7440477679377817601) and Sentiv commentary on **governance vs consumer WhatsApp**). Treat **Sentiv** as **integrator, reseller, and local trust anchor**.

## Partnerships (SA)

- **Sentiv:** See **[Sentiv.md](./Sentiv.md)** — bundled positioning for **secure, sovereign, administrable** messaging vs **shadow IT** on **WhatsApp** in **public safety** and regulated contexts. Cited **EV-2026-03-006**.

## Vs **Wyzetalk**

- **Overlap:** **Mobile-first** operational messaging; **emergency / urgent** style alerting; **government and infrastructure** accounts in **South Africa**; narrative against **uncontrolled WhatsApp** for sensitive and **evidence**-sensitive workflows; **frontline** field coordination.
- **Differentiation (their edge):** **Deepest** **European** **data-sovereignty** and **public-safety** feature set (SOS, silent-mode alerts, military/police case studies); **on-prem / German cloud**; **Teamwire + Sentiv** reaches buyers via **mission-critical comms** and **radio/CAD-adjacent** channels — not classic “EX intranet” RFPs.
- **Differentiation (our edge):** **Wyzetalk** **Essential** is a **full employee experience** surface — **feed, posts, groups, broader HR/comms**, **WhatsApp** as **engagement channel**, **payslip path**, **mining** and **regulated-industry** deskless story, and **coexistence** with **Teams/Workplace**. Teamwire is **not** a drop-in for **HR self-service** or **rich content employee engagement** unless the buyer only shops **secure messenger**. In **dual-track** procurements (EX app *and* sovereign ops messenger), expect **coexistence** or **adjacency** rather than single-vendor assumption.
- **Risk vector:** In **municipal, EMS, security, and rail-type** accounts, **Sentiv’s** footprint + **“more than a chat app”** narrative can **pre-empt** or **parallel** employee-comms decisions — especially where **audit trail** and **offboarding** dominate. **RFI language** may blur **secure messenger** vs **employee platform**; **disambiguate early** in discovery.

# Sentiv

## Snapshot

- **Category:** **Mission-critical communications platform & integrator** — design, build, and operate **secure communication networks** and **adaptive operational platforms** for **public safety**, **critical infrastructure**, **transport**, **mining**, and large **enterprise** in **sub-Saharan Africa**.
- **Focus:** **South Africa** headquarters with **Johannesburg, Cape Town, KwaZulu-Natal** footprint; **200+ business partners** across **SSA** (per company site). Heritage narrative: **founded 1965**, “**55+ years**” of critical-comms delivery — **rebranded from Altron Nexus** after **management buyout** (**May 2025**) from **JSE-listed Altron** ([Business Day](https://www.businessday.co.za/bd/companies/telecoms-and-technology/2025-05-27-altron-nexus-rebranded-to-sentiv-after-management-buyout/)).
- **Leadership (press):** **Louis Du Toit** — MD / CEO post-buyout voice on GTM; **Reshaad Sha** — **executive chair** (ex–Liquid Intelligent Technologies). Named executives appear on **LinkedIn** partnership thread.
- **Capabilities (from public positioning):** **TETRA / DMR** radio ecosystems, **LTE** and **hybrid** comms, **command & control / CAD**, **surveillance**, **IoT**, **industrial automation / SCADA**-class messaging — **federated**, **tamper-proof**, **immutable chain-of-evidence** language for high-stakes ops.
- **Named reference angles (site testimonials):** **Namdeb** (TETRA + SafeMobile AVL), **Johannesburg EMS** (PTT / emergency coordination), other **municipal and healthcare** stories — use for **RFP triangulation**, not unverified win claims.

## Product & platform narrative

- **Platform:** “Intelligent, adaptive” layer that **integrates OEM/partner tech** via **APIs** rather than “just reselling silos” — positions **Sentiv** as **orchestrator** for **mission-critical** stacks.
- **People & purpose:** Heavy emphasis on **life-safety**, **public–private** security bridge, and **continuity** in **no-signal** or **crisis** environments (mines, airports, emergency services).
- **Differentiation vs consumer chat:** Leadership and partner commentary stresses **governance**, **audit**, **data sovereignty**, **offboarding**, and **evidence integrity** — explicitly contrasting **WhatsApp** use for **CPF / case-related** or **corporate IP** flows (see thread under [partnership post](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7440477679377817601)).

## Partnerships (Teamwire)

- **Teamwire:** **2026** GTM — **Sentiv** announces **partnership** with **[Teamwire](./Teamwire.md)** / [teamwire.eu](https://www.teamwire.eu/en/) to bring **“mission-critical communication infrastructure”** to **South African** organisations: **public safety**, **emergency response**, **municipal services**, **frontline operations** — positioning **Teamwire** as **purpose-built** vs **consumer chat**, with **centralised admin**, **sovereign hosting**, **high availability** ([Louis Du Toit LinkedIn activity](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7440477679377817601)). **Implication:** **Local enterprise + government route-to-market** for **EU secure messenger**; strengthens **“governed messaging”** story in **SA** next to **WhatsApp-heavy** HR players (**[Jem](./Jem.md)**, etc.) and **full EX** platforms (**Wyzetalk**).

## Vs **Wyzetalk**

- **Overlap:** **South African** buyer relationships in **government-adjacent**, **infrastructure**, **mining**, **logistics**; **frontline** coordination; **crisis and operational** comms narratives; anti–**shadow IT WhatsApp** messaging in **security-aware** accounts.
- **Differentiation (their edge):** **Deep legacy** in **radio, CAD, PTT, TETRA**, and **national-scale** critical infrastructure **integration**; **MBO** independence; **Teamwire** adds **modern encrypted messenger** with **EU** compliance **marketing** — **bundle** can look like **end-to-end critical comms** to a **CIO / security / municipal** buyer.
- **Differentiation (our edge):** **Wyzetalk** targets **employee experience** — **content**, **engagement**, **HR adjacency**, **WhatsApp channel** for **reach**, **Essential** SKU clarity — not primarily **LMR/TETRA** or **CAD** replacement. Where the buyer only wants **sovereign squad comms**, Teamwire/Sentiv may win **that** slice; where the buyer wants **workforce engagement + HR + safety comms in one employee surface**, **scope match** favors **Wyzetalk**. **Partner** positioning: we can **coexist** where **Teamwire** handles **governed ops messenger** and **WS** handles **EX** — only **competitive** if budget forces **single vendor** or **RFP collapses categories**.

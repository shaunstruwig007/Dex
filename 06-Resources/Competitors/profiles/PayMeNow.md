# PayMeNow

## Snapshot

- **Category:** Earned wage access (EWA) / financial wellbeing; expanding **employee touchpoints** (incl. WhatsApp) for workforce engagement in South Africa and broader Africa.
- **Focus:** Frontline and deskless-friendly — mobile money, payroll integration, POPIA-aware positioning; **WhatsApp** as a channel for support and engagement alongside app/web.

## Vs [[Wyzetalk]]

- **Overlap:** Reaching employees on devices they already use (incl. WhatsApp); employer-branded employee experience; operational comms adjacent to pay/financial wellbeing.
- **Differentiation:** [[Wyzetalk]] is broader **EX platform** (comms, safety, digitisation, engagement, analytics) vs PayMeNow’s core around **wages and financial access** — compete/coexist depending on RFP (pure IC vs financial wellbeing bundle). Triangulate when buyers conflate “WhatsApp employee app” with full EX.

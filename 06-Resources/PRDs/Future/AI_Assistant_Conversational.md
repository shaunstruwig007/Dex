# Discovery — AI assistant & conversational self-service

> **Status:** Pre-PRD discovery stub · **Phase:** Next (post–Essential GA)

## Hypothesis

Frontline workers need **answers** (policies, HR, eventually **leave** and transactions) via **WhatsApp** and/or an **in-app chatbot**, grounded in **approved** company criteria—not brittle free text.

## Scope indicators (to validate)

- Intent routing; policy / knowledge retrieval; HR data handoff where integrated  
- **In-app** assistant shares an **intent graph** with WhatsApp where sensible  
- Escalation when confidence low or human required
